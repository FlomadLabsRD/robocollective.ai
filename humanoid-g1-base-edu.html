RoboCo G1 – Affordable, High-Performance Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot for Research and Education

The Unitree G1 humanoid robot is a next-generation bipedal robotics platform that combines affordability, agility, and advanced performance. With up to 43 Degrees of Freedom (DOF), high-torque joints, and force-controlled dexterous hands, the G1 replicates complex human motions including kicking, flipping, sprinting, and climbing, making it one of the most versatile humanoid robots in its class.

Engineered for research, education, and light industrial automation, the Unitree G1 humanoid robot is equipped with 3D LiDAR sensors, depth cameras, microphones, and speakers for intelligent navigation and real-time interaction. Its modular design allows configuration in three versions — Base, Edu, and Ultimate — offering scalable capabilities that adapt to different applications and budgets.

Dynamic Motion and Power

The G1 features 120 Nm torque-dense joints and a lightweight frame (~35 kg), delivering smooth, stable locomotion across complex environments. Its design supports human-like dexterity and motion control, making it ideal for universities, research labs, and innovation centers exploring humanoid locomotion and robotic collaboration.

Advanced Sensors and Perception

Equipped with advanced sensor arrays, the Unitree G1 humanoid robot ensures accurate spatial awareness and interaction:

  • 3D LiDAR sensor for 360° panoramic mapping

  • Intel RealSense D435i depth camera for advanced perception

  • Integrated microphones and speakers for communication and interactive AI projects

These features enable AI-based navigation, real-time collaboration, and adaptive behavior in both classroom and research environments.

Modular Design and Flexibility

The G1’s modular configurations (Base, Edu, Ultimate) make it suitable for a wide range of uses, from entry-level robotics education to advanced AI-driven research. Quick-swap batteries provide ~2 hours of runtime, ensuring uninterrupted learning, training, or demonstrations.

Key Specifications of the Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot

  • Height: ~130 cm

  • Weight: ~35 kg

  • Degrees of Freedom: 43 DOF

  • Maximum Joint Torque: 120 Nm

  • Battery Life: ~2 hours (quick-swap system)

  • Sensors: 3D LiDAR + Intel RealSense D435i depth camera

  • Configurations: Base, Edu, Ultimate

Why Choose the Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot?

Backed by cutting-edge mobility, advanced perception, and modular scalability, the Unitree G1 humanoid robot is the perfect platform for:

  • AI testing and robotics education

  • Research and development in humanoid locomotion

  • Automation training and industrial demonstrations

With its combination of affordability, performance, and adaptability, the Unitree G1 is redefining the standard for humanoid robotics in academic and industrial environments.
