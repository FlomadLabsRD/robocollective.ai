RML63-B 6-DOF Long Arm Span Robotic Arm – Basic Version

The RML63-B is a versatile six-degree-of-freedom robotic arm created for businesses and research institutions that need a cost-effective, high-performance automation solution. With its lightweight, compact, and flexible design, it simplifies installation and saves valuable space. The modular joint structure ensures adaptability, making the RML63-B suitable for a wide range of industrial, medical, and service applications.

Powerful Performance

Although it weighs only 10 kg, the RML63-B delivers remarkable performance. It supports a payload capacity of 3 kg and a working radius of 900 mm, offering both reach and strength for demanding operations. The arm achieves a repeatability of ±0.05 mm, ensuring consistent accuracy in repetitive tasks.

Energy-Efficient and Durable

Powered by DC 24V with a low maximum consumption of just 200 W, the RML63-B is exceptionally energy-efficient. In addition, its IP54-rated aluminum alloy construction shields the arm from dust and splashes, guaranteeing reliability in diverse environments.

Easy Integration

The RML63-B is built with usability in mind. It supports drag-and-drop teaching, pendant control, and API/JSON integration. Furthermore, it offers multiple connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB, and RS485. With joint speeds reaching 225°/s, the arm executes dynamic operations quickly and efficiently.

Long-Term Value

Whether applied in industrial production, medical automation, intelligent inspection, or health and wellness solutions, the RML63-B combines precision, flexibility, and affordability. Backed by a service life exceeding 30,000 hours, it provides organizations with a reliable long-term investment, helping them expand automation while maintaining control of costs.
