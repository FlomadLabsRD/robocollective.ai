Not ready to sell? Rent out your robots

Sometimes it makes more sense to keep a robot in your fleet and let it earn money between projects. If you have platforms that sit idle part of the year, you can list them for rentals on RoboCollective.ai instead of selling them outright.

How renting out your robots works

  1. List your robot
    Share model details, condition, payloads, and where the robot is located. You stay in control of availability, pricing, and what types of use are allowed.
  2. Match and schedule
    We promote your listing to companies, universities, and event organizers who need short term robotics—for demos, pilots, launches, and seasonal peaks.
  3. Support and payout
    We help coordinate logistics, basic onboarding guidance, and return checks. After each completed rental, you receive payment according to the agreed terms.
