RoboCo Heavy Load AMR – Smart, High-Capacity Autonomous Mobile Robot

The RoboCo Heavy Load AMR is a next-generation autonomous mobile robot designed to carry payloads of up to 600 kg. Built for factories, warehouses, and logistics, it combines strength, precision, and adaptability. As a result, it has become the preferred solution for businesses looking to reduce labor costs and improve efficiency with reliable automation.

High Payload and Smart Lifting

Thanks to its advanced lifting system, the Heavy Load AMR can identify shelves, transport goods, and place items with precision. This automation reduces manual effort, lowers operational risks, and boosts productivity. Moreover, with a rapid charging time of 1.1 hours and an 8-hour battery endurance, it supports continuous operations without delays.

Seamless Integration and Scheduling

The AMR integrates smoothly with MES, ERP, and WMS platforms, allowing real-time task assignment. In addition, its multi-robot scheduling (RCS) ensures multiple robots can coordinate effectively in large-scale facilities. Therefore, scaling up operations becomes simple and cost-effective.

Built for Global Standards

Certified under CE, FCC, KC, NCC, and EMC, the RoboCo Heavy Load AMR meets the highest safety and quality standards. It also supports 4G/Wi-Fi connectivity, enabling easy integration with IoT devices, barcode scanners, call buttons, elevators, and cloud-based monitoring. Furthermore, its expandable peripherals—such as notification lights, follow bracelets, and rollers—allow maximum customization.

Proven Worldwide

Already deployed in 50+ countries and trusted by over 1,500 enterprises, RoboCo robots deliver measurable results. Businesses worldwide rely on the Heavy Load AMR to cut costs, increase productivity, and future-proof their automation strategies. With its high lifting capacity, advanced navigation, and rapid deployment, it is the ultimate choice for modern smart factories.
