Intel RealSense D435C 3D Camera – Depth & RGB Vision for Robotics and AI

The D435C is a high-performance 3D camera that combines depth sensing, RGB color imaging, and VCSEL projector technology to deliver accurate perception even in challenging environments. Built with a global shutter sensor and a large lens, it ensures crisp, distortion-free images with excellent low-light performance, making it an ideal solution for robotics, AI, AR/VR, and automation projects.

With a working distance of 0.2 m to 10 m, the D435C is versatile enough for both close-range interactions and extended-range detection. It provides a depth resolution of 1280 × 720 at 90 fps for smooth and detailed depth mapping, and a color resolution of 1920 × 1080 at 30 fps for high-quality RGB video. Its wide depth FOV of 87° × 58° × 95° and color FOV of 69.4° × 42.5° × 77° ensure a broad field of vision for capturing dynamic scenes.

Engineered for accuracy, the D435C maintains a relative error of only 2% at 2000 mm, ensuring reliable 3D measurement and object detection. Despite its advanced capabilities, the camera remains compact at 90 × 25 × 25 mm and integrates seamlessly via USB 3.0 with a standard 5V power supply.

Whether you are building autonomous robots, interactive AI systems, or computer vision solutions, the D435C provides the precision, speed, and reliability needed to bring your innovations to life.
