Industrial Smart Lifting Robot – L Series

The L Series Industrial Smart Lifting Robot is a next-generation material-handling solution built to optimize logistics, warehousing, and manufacturing workflows. Designed for rapid deployment, it sets up in minutes and delivers automated lifting, transport, and shelf-handling functions with exceptional reliability.

Intelligent Lifting Power

Using advanced recognition, the L Series can autonomously identify shelves and adapt to different workflows. Depending on the model, it supports load capacities between 150–600 kg. With precision positioning accuracy of 10 mm and navigation modes such as free navigation, track navigation, hybrid navigation, and follow mode, it ensures smooth operation even in complex factory layouts.

Seamless System Integration

The robot integrates effortlessly with existing MES, ERP, and WMS systems, enabling smart task assignment and centralized management. In addition, advanced RCS multi-robot scheduling allows multiple robots to operate together for maximum throughput.

Reliable, Long-Lasting Performance

Powered by a 12-hour endurance battery (with 5-hour fast charging), the L Series provides consistent uptime. Its robust build is backed by CE, FCC, KC, and EMC certifications, ensuring compliance with global safety standards. The robot also supports auto-charging and elevator compatibility, extending its usability across multi-floor environments.

Scalable and Future-Proof

With expandability at its core, the L Series connects to IoT devices, call systems, RFID, barcode scanners, and cloud-based data platforms. Trusted by 1,500+ enterprises across 50+ countries, it has already proven to reduce labor costs, increase productivity, and future-proof smart factories.
