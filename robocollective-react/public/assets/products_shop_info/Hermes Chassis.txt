<h1>Hermes</h1>
The Hermes robot platform is equipped with a navigation and positioning core independently developed by Slam, as well as a 360° LiDAR and two depth camera systems. It has excellent autonomous mobility and stable positioning and navigation capabilities and can independently take elevators to achieve multi-floor accessible mobility.

<strong>Navigation mode</strong>
-Laser navigation

<strong>Size (length x width x height)</strong>
545 x 465 x 272mm

<strong>Self-weight</strong>
40kg

<strong>Rated load</strong>
50kg

<strong>Maximum load</strong>
55kg

<strong>Maximum speed</strong>
1m/s

<strong>Maximum threshold-crossing</strong>
20mm

<strong>Maximum climbing</strong>
10°

<strong>Maximum ditch-crossing</strong>
40mm

<strong>Movement accuracy</strong>
±5cm

<strong>Rotation radius</strong>
Rotate in place

<strong>Battery capacity</strong>
25.2V，32Ah

<strong>Control interface</strong>
TCP socket (Ethernet/WiFi)

<strong>Sensors</strong>
LiDAR, 3D visual sensor, magnetic sensor, collision avoidance sensor

<strong>Function</strong>
Scan to create images and automatically recharge
