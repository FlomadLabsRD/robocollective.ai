ECO65-B Robotic Arm – Affordable 6-DOF Automation with High Payload

The ECO65-B is a six-degree-of-freedom (6-DOF) economical robotic arm from RealMan. It is designed for companies and research labs that need a balance of precision, strength, and affordability. Built with an integrated controller and durable housing, the ECO65-B is well-suited for logistics, 3C electronics, industrial production, and specialized automation.

Compact and Strong

Despite its compact size, the ECO65-B delivers impressive performance. It supports a payload capacity of up to 5 kg and a working radius of 610 mm. With a repeat positioning accuracy of ±0.05 mm, it ensures consistent results in demanding environments. Weighing only 7.8 kg, it is easy to install and integrate into different setups. The IP54-rated housing adds protection against dust and splashes, extending its service life.

Efficient and Reliable

Powered by DC 20–30V, the ECO65-B consumes only 200 W, making it an energy-efficient choice. Safety features include hard brakes for J1–J2 and soft brake protection for other joints, ensuring smooth and secure operation.

Flexible Control and Connectivity

The robotic arm supports Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB, RS485, and network interfaces. Programming is simplified through drag-and-drop teaching, pendant control, API integration, and JSON compatibility. This makes deployment fast, adaptable, and user-friendly.

A Smart Investment

With joint speeds up to 225°/s and a motion range of ±178°, the ECO65-B combines speed with accuracy. It offers an affordable, high-performance automation solution for organizations seeking to expand capabilities while controlling costs.
