Unitree H1 Humanoid Robot – Dynamic Motion and Power

With 360 Nm torque-dense joints, the Unitree H1 humanoid robot delivers advanced movements such as running, jumping, and flips. Its strong design ensures stable locomotion in challenging environments, setting new benchmarks for humanoid mobility research and development.

Advanced Perception and Sensing

Equipped with cutting-edge sensors, the H1 humanoid robot offers reliable perception for AI-driven projects:

  • 3D LiDAR sensor for panoramic spatial mapping

  • Intel RealSense D435i depth camera for advanced 360° awareness

These enable AI-based navigation, human-robot interaction, and environmental awareness, making the H1 ideal for autonomous robotics research.

Research-Grade AI Platform

Powered by Intel i-Series or NVIDIA Jetson processors, the Unitree H1 supports embodied AI experiments, reinforcement learning, and adaptive robotics automation. With a maximum speed of 5 m/s and swappable battery packs, it is optimized for long-duration missions and high-intensity research projects.

Key Specifications

  • Height: 152 cm (5 ft)

  • Weight: 47 kg (103 lbs)

  • Torque: 360 Nm joints

  • Max Speed: 5 m/s

  • Sensors: 3D LiDAR + Intel RealSense D435i

  • Power: Swappable batteries for extended runtime

Why Choose the Unitree H1 Humanoid Robot?

By uniting raw power, agile mobility, and advanced sensing, the Unitree H1 humanoid robot is more than a research platform—it is a future-ready robotics solution for AI innovation, humanoid locomotion, and advanced automation.
