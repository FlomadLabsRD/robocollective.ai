<h2 data-start="596" data-end="688">Mini Pi Bipedal Robot – High-Performance, Compact, and Cost-Effective Robotics Platform</h2>
<p data-start="690" data-end="1020">The <strong data-start="694" data-end="719">Mini Pi Bipedal Robot</strong> is a next-generation humanoid platform built for <strong data-start="769" data-end="811">researchers, developers, and educators</strong> who need cutting-edge robotics at an affordable price. With <strong data-start="872" data-end="903">12 degrees of freedom (DOF)</strong> and precise joint actuation, it delivers stable, human-like walking and balance in a lightweight, portable design.</p>
