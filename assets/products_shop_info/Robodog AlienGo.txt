<h2 data-start="198" data-end="258"><strong data-start="201" data-end="258">Unitree AlienGo – Advanced Industrial Quadruped Robot</strong></h2>
<p data-start="260" data-end="524">The Unitree AlienGo is a high-performance quadruped robot designed for agility, intelligence, and real-world operation. It combines advanced sensing, high mobility, and strong durability, making it ideal for research, inspection, security, and rescue applications.</p>

<h3 data-start="526" data-end="546"><strong data-start="530" data-end="546">Key Features</strong></h3>
<ul data-start="547" data-end="1839">
 	<li data-start="547" data-end="743">
<p data-start="549" data-end="743"><strong data-start="549" data-end="572">Dynamic Locomotion:</strong> Equipped with 12 high-performance servo motors, AlienGo can run, move backward, sidestep, rotate in place, crawl, jump, roll over, and climb stairs or slopes with ease.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="744" data-end="878">
<p data-start="746" data-end="878"><strong data-start="746" data-end="768">Superior Mobility:</strong> Capable of achieving walking speeds above 1.5 m/s, ensuring fast and stable movement across varied terrain.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="879" data-end="1008">
<p data-start="881" data-end="1008"><strong data-start="881" data-end="906">Terrain Adaptability:</strong> Maintains precise control and balance on gravel, grass, uneven ground, and slopes up to 25 degrees.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1009" data-end="1167">
<p data-start="1011" data-end="1167"><strong data-start="1011" data-end="1029">Durable Build:</strong> Constructed from polymer composites, carbon fiber, and aviation-grade aluminum for excellent impact resistance and mechanical strength.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1168" data-end="1309">
<p data-start="1170" data-end="1309"><strong data-start="1170" data-end="1194">Advanced Perception:</strong> Features dual depth cameras, a visual odometry camera, and optional LiDAR for 3D mapping and obstacle detection.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1310" data-end="1447">
<p data-start="1312" data-end="1447"><strong data-start="1312" data-end="1337">Smart Control System:</strong> Offers real-time visual SLAM, 3D environmental awareness, and minimal latency for smooth autonomous motion.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1448" data-end="1601">
<p data-start="1450" data-end="1601"><strong data-start="1450" data-end="1474">Flexible Interfaces:</strong> Includes Ethernet, USB, and RS-485 ports, plus multiple voltage outputs for attaching sensors, robotic arms, or GPS modules.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1602" data-end="1710">
<p data-start="1604" data-end="1710"><strong data-start="1604" data-end="1627">Developer-Friendly:</strong> Supports C/C++ and ROS, allowing full-level control and algorithm customization.</p>
</li>
 	<li data-start="1711" data-end="1839">
<p data-start="1713" data-end="1839"><strong data-start="1713" data-end="1744">Strong Payload &amp; Endurance:</strong> Carries up to 13 kg of payload and operates for 2.5–4.6 hours depending on load and terrain.</p>
</li>
</ul>
