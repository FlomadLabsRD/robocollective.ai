<h1 data-start="267" data-end="364">RoboCo G1 – Affordable, High-Performance Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot for Research and Education</h1>
<p data-start="366" data-end="786">The <strong data-start="370" data-end="399">Unitree G1 humanoid robot</strong> is a next-generation bipedal robotics platform that combines affordability, agility, and advanced performance. With up to <strong data-start="522" data-end="553">43 Degrees of Freedom (DOF)</strong>, <strong data-start="555" data-end="577">high-torque joints</strong>, and <strong data-start="583" data-end="619">force-controlled dexterous hands</strong>, the G1 replicates complex human motions including <strong data-start="671" data-end="717">kicking, flipping, sprinting, and climbing</strong>, making it one of the most versatile humanoid robots in its class.</p>
<p data-start="788" data-end="1201">Engineered for <strong data-start="803" data-end="859">research, education, and light industrial automation</strong>, the Unitree G1 humanoid robot is equipped with <strong data-start="908" data-end="970">3D LiDAR sensors, depth cameras, microphones, and speakers</strong> for intelligent navigation and real-time interaction. Its <strong data-start="1029" data-end="1047">modular design</strong> allows configuration in three versions — <strong data-start="1089" data-end="1116">Base, Edu, and Ultimate</strong> — offering scalable capabilities that adapt to different applications and budgets.</p>

<h2 data-start="1208" data-end="1237">Dynamic Motion and Power</h2>
<p data-start="1238" data-end="1576">The G1 features <strong data-start="1254" data-end="1284">120 Nm torque-dense joints</strong> and a lightweight frame (~35 kg), delivering smooth, stable locomotion across complex environments. Its design supports human-like dexterity and motion control, making it ideal for universities, research labs, and innovation centers exploring humanoid locomotion and robotic collaboration.</p>
