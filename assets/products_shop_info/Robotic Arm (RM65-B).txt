The RM65-B, a basic version of the 6-dof robotic arm, is self-developed and produced by RealMan. The robotic arm integrating the controller weighs 7.2KG in total, with a payload of 5KG, a peak load of 9KG, and a working radius of 610mm.

<strong>Degree of freedom</strong>
6

<strong>Payload</strong>
5kg

<strong>Body weight</strong>
7.2kg

<strong>Repeatability</strong>
±0.05mm

<strong>Working radius</strong>
610mm

<strong>Power supply voltage</strong>
DC24V

<strong>Power consumption</strong>
Maximum power consumption 200W, comprehensive power consumption 100W

<strong>Protection level</strong>
IP54(robotic arm body)

<strong>Material</strong>
Aluminum alloy

<strong>Controller</strong>
Integrated

<strong>Communication mode</strong>
WIFI/network port/Bluetooth/USB serial port/RS485

<strong>Control mode</strong>
Drag and drop teaching/Teaching pendant/API/JSON

<strong>Joint motion range</strong>
J1 ±178°、J2 ±130°、J3 ±135°、J4 ±178°、J5 ±128°、J6 ±360°

<strong>Joint maximum speed</strong>
J1-J2 180°/s、J3-J6 225°/s
