<h3 data-start="201" data-end="213">Overview</h3>
<p data-start="214" data-end="592">The <strong data-start="218" data-end="233">Unitree Go2</strong> is a next-generation quadruped robot that blends advanced mobility, intelligent AI, and real-time sensing technologies. It’s designed as a consumer-grade embodiment of robotic innovation, offering dynamic functionality across personal, educational, research, and light commercial applications.</p>

<h3 data-start="594" data-end="610">Key Features</h3>
<ul data-start="612" data-end="2835">

<p data-start="614" data-end="866"><strong data-start="614" data-end="647">Embodied AI &amp; GPT Integration</strong><br data-start="647" data-end="650" />Infused with large-model GPT-style AI, the Go2 delivers interactive and intuitive experiences, simulating behaviors reminiscent of a real pet while being powerfully responsive</p>
</li>

<p data-start="870" data-end="1154"><strong data-start="870" data-end="903">4D Ultra-Wide LIDAR L1 System</strong><br data-start="903" data-end="906" />Equipped with Unitree’s proprietary 4D LIDAR L1, the Go2 offers 360°×90° hemispherical scanning, near-zero blind spots, and detection capabilities as close as 0.05 m—ideal for precise all-terrain recognition</p>
</li>
