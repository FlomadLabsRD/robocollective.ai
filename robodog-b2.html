Unitree B2 – Industrial-Grade Quadruped Robot

The Unitree B2 represents a major leap in industrial-grade robotic mobility. Designed for all-terrain adaptability, high payload capacity, and long-duration missions, it redefines how enterprises approach inspection, maintenance, and rescue operations.

Key Features

  • High Speed: Capable of running over 6 m/s, setting a new benchmark in quadruped performance.

  • Extreme Agility: Navigates stairs, steep slopes over 45°, and obstacles up to 40 cm with ease.

  • Heavy Payload: Supports up to 120 kg standing load and 40 kg continuous walking load.

  • Long Endurance: Operates over 5 hours and 20 km without load, or 4 hours and 15 km carrying 20 kg.

  • Powerful Joints: High-torque actuators deliver up to 360 N·m, ensuring stable, dynamic motion.

  • Hybrid Design: Optional wheeled-legged structure extends endurance and mobility range.

  • Autonomous Charging: Plug-in battery enables quick swaps and automatic charging.

  • Rugged Build: IP67 protection with an operating range from -20 °C to 55 °C.

  • Advanced Perception: Equipped with 3D LiDAR, depth and optical cameras, plus multiple industrial I/O ports.

Specifications

  • Standing Size: 1098 × 450 × 645 mm

  • Prone Size: 880 × 460 × 330 mm

  • Weight: ≈ 60 kg (with battery)

  • Battery: 45 Ah / 2250 Wh / 58 V

  • Max Jump Distance: > 1.6 m

Applications

  • Power-Line and Facility Inspection: Automates routine and high-risk maintenance tasks.

  • Emergency Rescue: Performs in rough, unstable environments like rubble or steep terrain.

  • Industrial Monitoring: Integrates mapping, infrared sensing, and 3D perception for automated inspection.

  • Outdoor Operations: Performs reliably across construction, logistics, and infrastructure sites.

Why Choose Unitree B2

Delivering two to three times the performance of conventional quadruped robots, the Unitree B2 combines strength, stability, and smart adaptability. It’s a versatile platform built for real-world missions where endurance, payload, and mobility truly matter.
