RML63-6F 6-DOF Long Arm Span Robotic Arm – Precision, Force Sensing, and Versatility

The RML63-6F robotic arm is a next-generation 6-DOF automation solution designed for industries that demand accuracy, flexibility, and adaptability. With its integrated 6-axis force sensing and bottom-mounted controller, this robotic arm delivers exceptional precision for advanced automation in manufacturing, healthcare, and research.

High Payload and Extended Reach

With a payload capacity of 3 kg and a working radius of 928.5 mm, the RML63-6F provides extended reach for complex workflows. Its six-axis force range of 200N/7Nm and ±0.05 mm repeatability ensure reliable performance in sensitive operations such as medical sample management, elderly care, and delicate industrial handling.

Durable and Efficient Design

Built from aluminum alloy with an IP54 protection rating, the arm withstands diverse environments. Its joint motion range of ±178° and full 360° rotation deliver smooth, responsive operation at speeds of 225°/s. With DC24V power and ≤200W consumption, it offers energy efficiency while supporting Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB, and RS485 connectivity.

Easy Programming and Integration

The RML63-6F supports drag-and-drop teaching, API/JSON programming, and pendant control, making it user-friendly for both beginners and advanced developers. Backed by a 30,000-hour service life, it ensures long-term value across applications in industrial production, healthcare, and smart automation.

By combining performance, intelligence, and adaptability, the RML63-6F 6-DOF robotic arm sets a new standard for collaborative and force-sensing robotics.
