RealMan Single Arm Compound Robot – Precision, Mobility, and Versatility

The RealMan Single Arm Compound Robot is a next-generation automation solution that combines a high-performance robotic arm with a mobile platform. Designed for industries that demand flexibility and accuracy, it delivers unmatched efficiency for logistics, manufacturing, healthcare, and service applications.

High-Performance Robotic Arm

Built on RealMan’s humanoid arm technology, the robot features six degrees of freedom (6-DOF), exceptional repeatability of ±0.05 mm, and a high payload capacity. As a result, it performs precision tasks such as inspection, handling, and human-robot collaboration with ease.

Smart Mobility and Navigation

Mounted on a highly maneuverable autonomous base, the robot adapts to dynamic environments without requiring infrastructure changes. Moreover, its compact footprint allows smooth operation in limited spaces while maintaining energy-efficient performance.

Easy Integration and Control

The modular design supports drag-and-drop teaching, API/JSON integration, and wireless connectivity via Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and RS485. Therefore, deployment is fast, and integration with existing workflows is simple. In addition, its modular joints make it scalable for evolving business needs.

Scalable and Cost-Effective

By combining mobility with robotic dexterity, the RealMan Single Arm Compound Robot helps companies reduce labor costs, increase productivity, and future-proof their operations. Trusted for its adaptability, it sets a new benchmark in collaborative robotics.
