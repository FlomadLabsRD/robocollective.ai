Unitree AlienGo – Advanced Industrial Quadruped Robot

The Unitree AlienGo is a high-performance quadruped robot designed for agility, intelligence, and real-world operation. It combines advanced sensing, high mobility, and strong durability, making it ideal for research, inspection, security, and rescue applications.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Locomotion: Equipped with 12 high-performance servo motors, AlienGo can run, move backward, sidestep, rotate in place, crawl, jump, roll over, and climb stairs or slopes with ease.

  • Superior Mobility: Capable of achieving walking speeds above 1.5 m/s, ensuring fast and stable movement across varied terrain.

  • Terrain Adaptability: Maintains precise control and balance on gravel, grass, uneven ground, and slopes up to 25 degrees.

  • Durable Build: Constructed from polymer composites, carbon fiber, and aviation-grade aluminum for excellent impact resistance and mechanical strength.

  • Advanced Perception: Features dual depth cameras, a visual odometry camera, and optional LiDAR for 3D mapping and obstacle detection.

  • Smart Control System: Offers real-time visual SLAM, 3D environmental awareness, and minimal latency for smooth autonomous motion.

  • Flexible Interfaces: Includes Ethernet, USB, and RS-485 ports, plus multiple voltage outputs for attaching sensors, robotic arms, or GPS modules.

  • Developer-Friendly: Supports C/C++ and ROS, allowing full-level control and algorithm customization.

  • Strong Payload & Endurance: Carries up to 13 kg of payload and operates for 2.5–4.6 hours depending on load and terrain.

Specifications

  • Weight: 21.5 kg ± 1 kg

  • Dimensions (Standing): 650 mm × 310 mm × 600 mm

  • Dimensions (Folded): 600 mm × 310 mm × 150 mm

  • Walking Speed: > 1.5 m/s

  • Climbing Capability: ≤ 25°

  • Payload Capacity: ≈ 13 kg

  • Endurance: 2.5–4.6 hours

Applications

  • Industrial Inspection: Autonomous navigation for factory, tunnel, and power-plant inspection.

  • Security and Surveillance: Performs continuous patrols across complex indoor and outdoor environments.

  • Scientific Research: Ideal for robotics research in motion control, perception, and multi-robot coordination.

  • Rescue and Exploration: Handles uneven, unpredictable terrain in emergency or remote operations.

Why Choose Unitree AlienGo

AlienGo combines powerful motion performance, strong durability, and intelligent sensing in a compact quadruped form. With its open-source development support, industrial-grade design, and field-tested stability, AlienGo is a versatile robot platform built to perform in both research and real-world industrial applications.
