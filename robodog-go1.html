Overview

The Unitree Go1 is a next-generation quadruped robot that blends advanced mobility, intelligent AI, and real-time sensing technologies. It’s designed as a consumer-grade embodiment of robotic innovation, offering dynamic functionality across personal, educational, research, and light commercial applications.

Key Features

  • Embodied AI & GPT Integration
    Infused with large-model GPT-style AI, the Go1 delivers interactive and intuitive experiences, simulating behaviors reminiscent of a real pet while being powerfully responsive

  • 4D Ultra-Wide LIDAR L1 System
    Equipped with Unitree’s proprietary 4D LIDAR L1, the Go1 offers 360°×90° hemispherical scanning, near-zero blind spots, and detection capabilities as close as 0.05 m—ideal for precise all-terrain recognition

  • Mobility & Agility
    The Go1 can walk, trot, run up to 4.7m/s

  • Variations to Fit Your Needs
    Available in multiple versions:

    • Go1 Air: Lightweight, designed for daily interactive use

    • Go1 Pro: Enhanced with 4G connectivity, voice commands, and stronger computing

  • Robust Construction & Specs
    Measures approximately 70 × 31 × 40 cm standing (76 × 31 × 20 cm crouching), weighs around 15 kg (with battery), and features a durable build using aluminum alloy and high-strength engineering plastics.

  • Connectivity & Mapping
    Connectivity includes Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth, and (on select models) 4G/eSIM. The companion app supports 3D LiDAR mapping and autonomous navigation based on point-cloud paths.

  • Smart Features

    • ISS 2.0 (Intelligent Side-follow System): Enhances positioning accuracy by ~50% and boosts remote control range beyond 30m

    • OTA Upgrades: Built-in remote firmware updates via cloud ensure it continues to improve after purchase

  • Performance Metrics

    • Peak Joint Torque: Around 45 Nm

    • Battery Life: Typically 2–4 hours (depending on configuration and usage)

Use Cases & Market Position

The Unitree Go2 stands out as one of the most affordable, accessible quadruped robots offering rich functionality—traditionally limited to high-end or industrial platforms. Its key applications include:

  • Home & Entertainment: Acts as an intelligent companion, capable of playful, pet-like behaviors

  • Education & Research: Provides a platform for STEM learning and experimentation

  • Security & Inspection: Equipped for monitoring or light surveillance duties in varied environments
