RG52-050 Electric Rotating Gripper – Precision Handling with Smart Control

The RG52-050 is part of the advanced ERG series of electric rotating grippers, designed to deliver precision, flexibility, and control for robotic and automation applications. With its integrated drive and control system, the RG52-050 allows for adjustable and programmable force, position, and rotation angle, making it ideal for complex gripping and manipulation tasks. Its ability to rotate forward and backward infinitely adds versatility, while built-in features such as drop detection and gripping feedback ensure safe and reliable operation.

This model offers a clamping force of 2–50N and a generous travel range of up to 52 mm, enabling it to handle both delicate and moderately heavy components with ease. The opening/closing time of just 0.5s allows for efficient cycle times, while the position repeatability of ±0.02 mm ensures consistent accuracy for precision assembly and handling.

Compact yet powerful, the RG52-050 weighs only 0.85 kg and is easy to integrate into robotic systems. It operates on a 24V DC (±10%) power supply with a low-rated current of 0.4A, making it energy-efficient and reliable. For communication, it supports RS-485 (MODBUS RTU) and Digital I/O, ensuring seamless compatibility with industrial automation networks.

With a durable IP54 protection rating, the RG52-050 is resistant to dust and splashes, making it suitable for demanding environments. Whether you are working in electronics, precision assembly, packaging, or smart manufacturing, this gripper provides the accuracy, efficiency, and intelligent feedback needed to optimize your robotic operations.
