DaBai DCW Binocular Structured Light Camera – Precision Depth Sensing for Robotics & AI

The DaBai DCW is a professional-grade binocular structured light camera engineered to deliver high-precision depth perception and low-power performance for robotics, automation, and AI applications. Powered by self-developed ASIC chips, this camera ensures lower computing power requirements while providing high resolution, fast frame rates, and reliable obstacle detection across a wide field of vision.

With a working distance of 0.2 m to 2.5 m, the DaBai DCW is ideal for close-to-mid-range applications, such as indoor navigation, robotic grasping, and obstacle avoidance. Its depth FOV of H79° × V62° × D91° ± 3° ensures a wide capture angle, while the infrared FOV (16:9, H86° × V55° × D93.5° ± 3°) enhances depth accuracy in varying light conditions. The color FOV of H64° × V55° (4:3) provides clear, colorful imaging, making it suitable for vision-guided robotics and AI perception tasks.

The DaBai DCW supports a depth resolution of up to 1920 × 1080 at 5/10 fps, ensuring smooth data capture, and maintains relative accuracy of 1.0% at 1000 mm and 1.1% at 2000 mm. Despite its robust capabilities, it remains compact and lightweight at 89.82 × 25.10 × 25.10 mm, powered via a simple 5V USB 2.0 interface for easy integration.

Designed for robotics, navigation systems, and intelligent automation, the DaBai DCW strikes a balance between performance, efficiency, and portability. With average power consumption under 1.2 W, it’s the perfect solution for developers and researchers needing reliable 3D vision in compact robotic systems.
